# User Stories and Use Cases Document
**Let's Tour Zimbabwe Platform**

## Document Overview

### Purpose
This document provides comprehensive user stories and use cases for the Let's Tour Zimbabwe platform, detailing specific user interactions, workflows, and acceptance criteria for all stakeholder groups.

### Scope
- Primary user stories for schools, service providers, and platform administrators
- Secondary user stories for students, parents, and regulatory bodies
- Detailed use cases with step-by-step workflows
- Acceptance criteria and success metrics for each story

### User Story Format
```
As a [user type],
I want to [action/goal],
So that [benefit/outcome].

Acceptance Criteria:
- [Specific, testable criteria]
- [Additional criteria]

Priority: [High/Medium/Low]
Effort: [Small/Medium/Large]
```

## Primary User Stories

### Individual Service Providers (Priority Focus)

The platform prioritizes individual service providers to create an inclusive marketplace that empowers local entrepreneurs and ensures competitive pricing for schools. We focus on four key categories:

1. **Individual Chefs** - Local culinary professionals providing meal services
2. **Accommodation Providers** - Guesthouse owners, B&B operators, lodge managers
3. **Transport Providers** - Individual drivers with vehicles, small transport operators
4. **Individual Tour Guides** - Certified local guides with specialized knowledge

## Platform Core Features

### Location-Based Activity Management

The platform includes a comprehensive activity database organized by location/destination, enabling multi-city tour planning with accurate pricing and availability. This supports complex tour routes like:
- **Kadoma → Bulawayo → Hwange → Victoria Falls**
- **Harare → Great Zimbabwe → Matobo → Bulawayo**
- **Mutare → Chimanimani → Nyanga → Harare**

Each location has specific activities with estimated pricing, duration, capacity, and seasonal availability.

## Enhanced Use Cases

### Use Case 4: Multi-City Educational Tour Planning

**Primary Actor:** School Administrator  
**Goal:** Plan a comprehensive 5-day multi-city educational tour with location-specific activities  
**Preconditions:** Activity database populated with current pricing and availability

**Main Success Scenario:**

1. **Route Selection and Overview**
   - Administrator selects "Kadoma to Victoria Falls Educational Circuit" (5 days, 40 students)
   - System displays route: Kadoma → Bulawayo (Day 1) → Hwange (Day 2-3) → Victoria Falls (Day 4-5)
   - Views overview of available activities in each destination
   - Reviews total estimated cost: $1,950/student including activities

2. **Kadoma to Bulawayo (Day 1)**
   - **Departure Activity**: Kadoma Heritage Walk ($15/student, 2 hours)
   - **Travel**: Kadoma to Bulawayo (3 hours with cultural commentary)
   - **Arrival Activity**: Bulawayo Railway Museum ($12/student, 1.5 hours)
   - **Evening**: Check-in at Bulawayo Lodge, cultural dinner experience
   - **Day 1 Total**: $185/student

3. **Bulawayo to Hwange (Day 2)**
   - **Morning Activity**: Matobo National Park rock art tour ($35/student, 4 hours)
   - **Travel**: Bulawayo to Hwange (2.5 hours with wildlife spotting)
   - **Arrival**: Hwange Safari Lodge check-in
   - **Evening Activity**: Night game drive ($45/student, 3 hours)
   - **Day 2 Total**: $265/student

4. **Hwange National Park (Day 3)**
   - **Morning Activity**: Full day game drive with educational guide ($85/student, 8 hours)
   - **Afternoon Activity**: Conservation workshop at lodge ($25/student, 2 hours)
   - **Evening**: Traditional dinner and cultural performance
   - **Day 3 Total**: $195/student

5. **Hwange to Victoria Falls (Day 4)**
   - **Morning**: Travel to Victoria Falls (1.5 hours)
   - **Activity**: Victoria Falls guided tour ($40/student, 3 hours)
   - **Activity**: Zambezi River cultural cruise ($55/student, 2 hours)
   - **Evening**: Victoria Falls Hotel check-in and dinner
   - **Day 4 Total**: $285/student

6. **Victoria Falls Departure (Day 5)**
   - **Morning Activity**: Adventure activities (bungee/zip-line options) ($75/student, 3 hours)
   - **Activity**: Craft market cultural exchange ($20/student, 2 hours)
   - **Departure**: Return travel to Kadoma
   - **Day 5 Total**: $165/student

**Activity-Based Coordination:**
- Each activity automatically suggests relevant service providers
- **Hwange Game Drive** → connects with certified wildlife guides in Hwange
- **Victoria Falls Tour** → connects with guides specializing in geological/historical aspects
- **Cultural Activities** → connects with local cultural experts and performers
- **Accommodation** → automatically suggests properties near selected activities

**Success Criteria:**
- Complete 5-day multi-city itinerary planned in under 3 hours
- All activities properly sequenced with realistic travel times
- Total cost within budget parameters ($2,000/student target)
- All educational objectives covered across multiple subjects
- Service providers coordinated for seamless activity delivery

**Alternative Scenarios:**
- Activity unavailability: System suggests alternative activities in same location
- Budget constraints: System recommends lower-cost activities with similar educational value
- Weather concerns: System suggests indoor alternatives and seasonal activity swaps
- Group size issues: System adjusts activity selection based on capacity constraints

#### PA-001: Tour Transparency and Communication
**As a parent,**  
**I want transparent information about my child's educational tour,**  
**So that I can make informed decisions and stay connected during the trip.**

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Access detailed tour information including itinerary, costs, and safety measures
- View service provider credentials and safety certifications
- Receive regular updates and photos during the tour
- Access emergency contact information and communication channels
- Provide consent and medical information through secure forms
- Track tour progress and location updates (with privacy controls)
- Receive post-tour reports and educational outcomes
- Provide feedback on tour experience and suggestions for improvement

**Priority:** Low  
**Effort:** Medium  
**Dependencies:** Parent portal, communication system, privacy controls

## Detailed Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Complete Tour Planning Workflow

**Primary Actor:** School Administrator  
**Goal:** Plan and book a complete educational tour from discovery to confirmation  
**Preconditions:** User has valid school administrator account and tour requirements defined

**Main Success Scenario:**

1. **Tour Discovery Phase**
   - Administrator logs into platform and navigates to tour discovery
   - Sets search criteria: subject (History), budget ($2000/student), duration (3 days), group size (45 students)
   - Reviews 8 matching destinations with educational alignment scores
   - Selects "Great Zimbabwe Ruins Historical Tour" for detailed planning

2. **Itinerary Building Phase**
   - Uses drag-and-drop builder to create 3-day itinerary
   - Adds activities: guided ruins tour, archaeological workshop, cultural performance
   - Selects accommodation: Safari Lodge (2 nights)
   - Chooses transport: Luxury coach with certified driver
   - Reviews real-time pricing updates as selections are made

3. **Service Provider Selection Phase**
   - Compares 5 tour guide companies with ratings 4.2-4.8/5.0
   - Reviews detailed profiles, certifications, and customer feedback
   - Selects "Heritage Tours Zimbabwe" (4.7/5.0, 95% on-time record)
   - Compares 3 accommodation options and selects based on educational facilities

4. **Budget Optimization Phase**
   - Reviews total cost: $2,150/student (5% over initial budget)
   - Uses AI optimizer to find cost savings: alternative transport saves $75/student
   - Accepts optimized itinerary: $2,075/student (within budget)
   - Generates detailed budget breakdown for approval

5. **Booking and Confirmation Phase**
   - Submits booking request with 25% deposit requirement
   - Reviews and digitally signs service agreements
   - Receives booking confirmation with service provider contact details
   - Sets up payment schedule for remaining balance

**Success Criteria:**
- Complete tour planned within 2 hours
- All services booked with confirmed availability
- Budget optimized to stay within constraints
- All documentation generated and stored

**Alternative Scenarios:**
- Service provider unavailable: System suggests alternatives with similar ratings
- Budget exceeded: AI optimizer provides multiple cost-reduction options
- Compliance issues: System flags missing permits and provides guidance

### Use Case 2: Individual Service Provider Onboarding

**Primary Actor:** Individual Service Provider (Chef, Accommodation, Transport, or Tour Guide)  
**Goal:** Complete platform onboarding and achieve certified status as an individual provider  
**Preconditions:** Valid individual credentials and required documentation available

**Main Success Scenario:**

1. **Initial Registration**
   - Individual provider visits platform and selects provider type (Chef/Accommodation/Transport/Guide)
   - Completes personal information form with individual credentials
   - Uploads required documents: ID, relevant licenses, insurance, certifications
   - Creates initial service profile with basic offerings and pricing

2. **Provider-Specific Profile Development**
   - **Chef**: Uploads photos of dishes, kitchen facilities, menu samples
   - **Accommodation**: Uploads property photos, room layouts, facility details
   - **Transport**: Uploads vehicle photos, maintenance records, route expertise
   - **Tour Guide**: Uploads credentials, area expertise, educational qualifications
   - Sets availability calendar and pricing structure
   - Defines service capacity and geographic coverage

3. **Individual Verification Process**
   - Platform administrator reviews application within 24 hours
   - Automated checks verify individual licenses and insurance validity
   - Background checks conducted on the individual provider
   - Reference checks with previous clients (if applicable)
   - Skills assessment or interview for specialized roles (guides, chefs)

4. **Certification Achievement**
   - Completes role-specific online training modules
   - Passes assessment on educational tourism and safety standards
   - Receives "Certified Individual Provider" status with platform badge
   - Gains access to booking system and communication tools

5. **First Booking Success**
   - Receives first service request within 2 weeks of certification
   - Provides competitive quote using platform pricing guidelines
   - Coordinates with other providers for comprehensive tour package
   - Delivers exceptional individual service
   - Receives detailed feedback and rating from school

**Success Criteria:**
- Complete individual onboarding process within 3-5 days
- Achieve certified status within 1-2 weeks
- Receive first service request within 3 weeks
- Maintain 4.5+ star rating after first 3 service deliveries
- Successfully coordinate with other individual providers for complete tour packages

**Alternative Scenarios:**
- Documentation issues: System provides role-specific guidance and document templates
- Certification delays: Provider can operate with "Verified Individual" status while completing full certification
- Skills assessment concerns: Additional training modules and mentoring provided
- Coordination challenges: Platform facilitates introductions and collaboration between individual providers

### Use Case 3: Multi-Provider Tour Package Creation

**Primary Actor:** School Administrator  
**Secondary Actors:** Individual Chef, Accommodation Provider, Transport Provider, Tour Guide  
**Goal:** Create a complete tour package by coordinating multiple individual service providers  
**Preconditions:** All individual providers are certified and available for requested dates

**Main Success Scenario:**

1. **Tour Requirements Definition**
   - School administrator defines tour parameters: 35 students, 3 days, budget $1,800/student
   - Selects destination: Matobo National Park with cultural and historical focus
   - Specifies dietary requirements: 5 vegetarian, 2 halal, 3 gluten-free students
   - Sets dates: October 15-17, 2025

2. **Individual Provider Discovery**
   - Platform suggests individual providers in Matobo region:
     - **Chef**: Sarah Moyo (4.8/5, specializes in traditional cuisine, dietary accommodations)
     - **Accommodation**: Granite Lodge (4.6/5, educational facilities, group rooms)
     - **Transport**: James Ndlovu (4.9/5, luxury coach, cultural route expertise)
     - **Tour Guide**: Dr. Patricia Sibanda (4.7/5, archaeology PhD, Ndebele culture expert)

3. **Coordinated Quote Generation**
   - Each provider receives detailed requirements and creates individual quotes
   - Platform facilitates communication between providers for logistics coordination
   - **Chef Quote**: $45/student/day (includes all dietary accommodations)
   - **Accommodation Quote**: $65/student/night (educational group discount)
   - **Transport Quote**: $25/student (round trip with cultural commentary)
   - **Guide Quote**: $300/day total (specialized archaeological and cultural tours)

4. **Package Optimization and Booking**
   - Platform calculates total: $1,685/student (under budget by $115/student)
   - School administrator reviews and approves coordinated package
   - All providers confirm availability and accept bookings simultaneously
   - Platform generates master contract with individual provider agreements

5. **Pre-Tour Coordination**
   - Tour guide coordinates with chef on traditional meal experiences
   - Accommodation provider arranges educational spaces for evening activities
   - Transport provider plans route with guide for maximum educational impact
   - All providers receive shared communication channel for real-time coordination

**Success Criteria:**
- Complete package assembled within 4 hours of initial request
- All individual providers successfully coordinated without conflicts
- Total package cost within school's budget parameters
- All dietary and special requirements accommodated
- Seamless communication established between all parties

**Alternative Scenarios:**
- Provider unavailability: Platform suggests alternative individual providers with similar ratings
- Budget constraints: System recommends cost-saving alternatives while maintaining quality
- Coordination conflicts: Platform mediates scheduling and logistics discussions
- Last-minute changes: System facilitates rapid re-coordination between providers

**When:** Platform administrator reviews performance data  
**Then:** System should trigger quality improvement process and potential certification review

### Scenario 4: Peak Season Capacity Management
**Given:** High demand period with limited service provider availability  
**When:** Multiple schools compete for same services  
**Then:** System should manage fair allocation, suggest alternatives, and optimize overall satisfaction
